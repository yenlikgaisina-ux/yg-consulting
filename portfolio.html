Construction Sustainability Research Agent

“Can a multi-agent AI system autonomously research, analyse, and synthesise Scope 3 and embodied carbon intelligence for UK construction projects — reducing research time from days to minutes?”

Three-agent CrewAI pipeline (Researcher → Analyst → Writer) automates sustainability briefing production for UK construction teams. Agents use SerperDev search, web scraping, and Python tools to surface, validate, and synthesise current regulatory guidance from RICS, UKGBC, GHG Protocol, and BREEAM. Produces professional briefings with a validated metrics table, compliance deadlines, and concrete actions in under 10 minutes.

Construction Scope 3 Cost Forecasting

“How will Scope 3 emissions-related cost pressures evolve for UK construction subcontractors over the next 12–24 months?”

Forecasting carbon-driven cost pressures using ONS PPI and HMRC trade data. SARIMA, ETS Holt-Winters and OLS regression forecast material price indices; a Monte Carlo simulation (N=10,000) quantifies financial exposure for a representative £50M subcontractor.
