Who We Are

Tokbolat & Gaisina is a boutique consultancy at the intersection of sustainable construction, circular economy and artificial intelligence. We combine academic depth with practical project experience to turn data into decisions and ambitions into measurable impact.

Co-founder

Dr Serik Tokbolat

Serik is an Assistant Professor in Sustainability and Sustainable Construction Engineering at the University of Nottingham (acting here in an independent consulting capacity). His expertise includes sustainable construction, life cycle assessment (LCA), building energy modelling, building certification and circularity in construction.

He brings more than a decade of experience across academia and industry, supporting partners in their transition to net-zero and circular built environments.

Co-founder

Yenlik Gaisina, MPH

Yenlik is a sustainability, projects and data professional at the University of Nottingham, experienced in coordination of complex initiatives, community engagement and ESG-aligned strategy.

She is currently studying Data Analytics, AI and Machine Learning at the University of Cambridge, strengthening her ability to turn complex datasets into clear, actionable insights for decision-makers.

Services

AI-Driven Sustainability Strategy

  • Whole-life carbon models for projects and portfolios

Full Sustainability Strategy for the Built Environment

  • Carbon Footprinting – organisational, project and product footprints; LCA and whole-life carbon.
  • Monitoring & Reporting – KPIs, dashboards and data pipelines for ongoing performance tracking.
  • Whole-Life Costing – integrating capital, operational and end-of-life costs with carbon and circularity.
  • Circularity Assessment – material flows, reuse and recovery strategies, BIM-enabled circularity metrics.
  • Resilience Assessment (Climate-Proofing) – climate risk screening and adaptation strategies.
  • Certification & Compliance Support – gap analysis, documentation and submission support for building and environmental certification schemes.

Our Approach

Data-Driven by Design

We start with your data – BIM models, energy use, material quantities, costs and ESG indicators. Where data is missing, we help you design practical data-collection strategies.

AI, Machine Learning and Advanced Analytics

Depending on your project, we use tools such as:

  • Predictive models to forecast carbon, cost and performance
  • Clustering to segment assets or projects by risk and opportunity
  • Optimisation to identify high-impact, low-cost actions
  • Scenario simulations to explore “what if?” futures

We always translate technical outputs into clear language and visuals that boards, investors and project teams can act on.

Collaboration & Capacity Building

We co-create strategies with your teams and build internal capability so you can sustain change long after the project ends.

Training & Workshops

We design tailored training for executives, project teams, sustainability professionals and students.

  • Circular economy in construction
  • Whole-life carbon and LCA in practice
  • ESG for infrastructure and real estate
  • Data-driven sustainability: dashboards, KPIs and decision-making
  • Introduction to AI and machine learning for sustainability teams

Formats: 2-hour masterclasses, half-day workshops or multi-session programmes (online or in person).
