How It Works

From document to decision in minutes

The Copilot uses retrieval-augmented generation: your documents become the knowledge base, and every answer is grounded in that source material.

1

Upload your documents

Add PDFs, standards, policy documents, project guidance and reporting frameworks. The Copilot indexes them automatically using FAISS vector search.

2

Ask practical questions

Your engineers, consultants and project teams ask questions in plain English — no training required, no search queries to master.

3

Get grounded answers

The assistant retrieves relevant content from your documents and responds with practical, source-based guidance including the source reference.

4

Take action faster

Move from confusion to training, monitoring, reporting and project decisions in minutes. Generate checklists and escalate to a consultant when needed.
